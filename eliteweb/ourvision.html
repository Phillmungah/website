Our Vision

Elite aviation limited is aspiring to be 360 degree service provider in Aviation industry, we are committed towards achieving & ensuring Quality System which is integrated with aviation safety, security & environmental management system.

As complete Kenyan Business market including Aviation Industry is going through a transition period & experiencing robust growth ( which is one of its kind in world history) in consideration of opportunity creation. Elite aviation limited aims at ensuring that these opportunities must be seized & sized in the most effective way it can be API keeps an eye and holds all connection to ensure avoiding any cut losing in the execution period of growth plans.

Aviation vision:
As Elite aviation limited is basically dealing with the aviation Industry, though it does not stick to defining and confining itself to one cause, rather we here at EAL have a vision to be a complete service provider for A to N of Aviation.

Quality vision:
We are very much aware and put a disciplined process for quality control keeping the sensitivity of the Aviation Industry in mind and ensure that all our products supplied must comply with all quality control process before getting into your Aircraft. All products supplied by EAL acompany proper cetification such as FAA/EASA/ATA 106/PMA approval & others.

1. Quality is defined as conformance to requirement - in this regard we work with our customer & supplier ( both external & internal ) to define and achieve required.
2. We use risk management practises and prevention strategies to ensure application of the best quality system matched with aviation sector.
3. We measure quality costs & transfer it to customer, through discounted price.
